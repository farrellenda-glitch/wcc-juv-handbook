Whitehall Comcille's Games Promotion Officer (GPO) will keep mentors informed of all coaching and mentor development activities throughout the year via WhatsApp. Our GPO can also work with individual mentors and teams to help design coaching plans and sessions as required. We would encourage all mentors to actively enagage with our GPO.

Below are the standard/structured coaching courses that are available to all mentors in order of progression:


  1. Introduction to coaching gaelic games – The course is aimed at beginner coaches and will enable participants to assist a coach in the organisation of activities to develop players in both codes. It is expected that all Club Coaches and Mentors complete this at a minimum.

  2. Award 1 Child – This course is for coaches of Juvenile Teams (U12 and below) who have completed the ICGG course above. Candidates must be 16 years or over. Candidates will be asked to abide by the GAA Code of Best Practice in Youth Sport.

  3. Award 1 Youth and Adult – This course is for coaches of Juvenile Teams (U13 to adult teams) who have completed the ICGG course above. Candidates must be 18 years or over. Candidates will be asked to abide by the GAA Code of Best Practice in Youth Sport.

  4. Award 2 Child – This course is the third award on the coaching pathway of Gaelic games. The course is aimed at coaches that have progressed through the ICGG Award and Award 1 programmes and are currently coaching at CCC1 level.

  5. Award 2 Youth and Adult – This course is the third award on the coaching pathway of Gaelic games. The course is aimed at coaches that have progressed through the ICGG Award and Award 1 programmes and are currently coaching at CCC2/Adult level.
