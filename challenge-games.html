All Challenge Games need to be authorised by the county board. This authorisation starts by asking your section Secretary to submit the details below to the county board. This ensures all matches are covered by insurance. The county board will confirm by reply to the Secretary that all is in order.


The following details about the match should be included:

  • The Age Group eg. Under 13.
  • The Code eg. Hurling.
  • The Opposition and county if relevant eg. James Stephens, Kilkenny.
  • The Venue eg. Collins Avenue.
  • The Date and Time eg. 8th February 2026 at 3pm.
  • The Referee's Name eg. Danny Harrington.
