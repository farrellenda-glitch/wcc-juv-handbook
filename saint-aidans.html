1. Eircode – D09 XT54

  2. Google Maps Link – St. Aidans CBS

  3. Facilities – St. Aidans consists of a full size, adult grass pitch (slightly smaller than Collins Ave & Cloghran). There are dressing rooms and toilets on site. There are goalposts and flags on site to facilitate using the pitch for Juvenile games (not Go Games).

  4. Parking – St. Aidans has limited parking on site for approx 18 cars and is shared with St. Kevins when they are using the astro pitch. There is some parking across the road from the school but be mindful this is a residential area.
