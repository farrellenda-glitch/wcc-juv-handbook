CCC1 is the Dublin County Board body responsible for the organisation and administration of the younger juvenile age grades (Under 8 to Under 12) in Dublin GAA. It plays a central role in ensuring that games for children are run in a fair, structured and child-centred way. Under 8 to Under 11 operate on a Go Games blitz basis and graded individual team competitions start at Uunder 12.

Their key responsibilities are as follows:


  1. • Organises and administers juvenile games/competitions.
  2. • Plans and publishes fixtures across football and hurling.
  3. • Oversees team grading to ensure balanced and development-appropriate games.
  4. • Implements Go Games principles with an emphasis on participation and enjoyment.
  5. • Ensures compliance with age eligibility and competition regulations.
  6. • Manages rescheduling, walkovers, and competition-related issues.
  7. • Communicates rules, updates, and circulars to club juvenile secretary.
  8. • Handles objections or disputes relating to CCC1 competitions.
