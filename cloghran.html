  2. Google Maps Link – Cloghran Pitch

  3. Facilities – Cloghran consists of a full size, flood lit (training lights), grass, adult grass pitch. There are dressing rooms and toilets on site. There are goalposts and flags on site to facilitate using the pitch for Go Games and other Juvenile games.

  4. Parking – Cloghran has parking on site for 37 cars and can be busy particularly at times of change over between training and matches.
