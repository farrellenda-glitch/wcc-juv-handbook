  2. Google Maps Link – Lorcan Green

  3. Facilities – Lorcan Green is a grass training facility with temporary, chargeable flood lights for winter traning. It is approx 125m by 65m but doesn't have stop nets which limits its use.

  4. Parking – Parking is available onsite for approx 36 cars but is shared with the community centre. There is no parking outside as it is a very restricted residential area.
