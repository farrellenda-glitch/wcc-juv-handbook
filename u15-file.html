All under 15 teams in the club will participate in the Féile competitions in the first half of the year. There is a competition for every code. The formats across boys and girls differ with the boys competitions run over a number of months on a round robin group of 4 basis with a semi-finals and finals day in May. The girls . . .

Below are some of the key considerations for incoming under 15 mentors in preparing for the tournaments.


  1. Teams and panels – Each Féile competition has specific rules around naming fixed panels and the use of underage players. Please make sure all mentors understand these rules in detail.

  2. Fund raising – It is usual to have a fundraiser before Christmas or early in the year to raise money for Féile gear for your teams. This usually includes a top, shorts and socks for every player. A presentation of gear is organised by mentors prior to the first games.

  3. Hosting finals day/full blitz – The club may be asked or offered to host the finals day/or full blitz day. This will require much of the organisation details under PJ Troy and Beano Harte.
