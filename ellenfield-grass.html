1. Eircode – D09 T769

  2. Google Maps Link – Ellenfield Park

  3. Facilities – Ellenfield Park consists of 3 different sized grass pitches for use up to U16 games and training. All pitches can be reconfigured for Go Games set up with goalposts, nets and flags stored on site. There is a toilet in the pavillion at the Church Wall. Dublin City Council makes the call as to whether the pitches are playable in winter or not.

  4. Parking – Ellenfield is in a residential area so all WCC teams and visiting teams are asked to park in the Church Car Park.
