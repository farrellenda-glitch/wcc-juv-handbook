  2. Google Maps Link – Indoor Arena

  3. Facilities – The Indoor Arena is a training facility with an astro-type surface and targets on all walls that can be used by all ages for agiliy, S & C or ball work sessions. It has toilets on site.

  4. Parking – Parking is available onsite for approx 45 cars but is shared with the club house and Puck Lane. There is no parking outside as it is a very restricted residential area.
