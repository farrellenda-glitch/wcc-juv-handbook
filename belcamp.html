1. Eircode – D17 DW32

  2. Google Maps Link – Belcamp (Pitch 3)

  3. Facilities – Belcamp Pitch 3 is a full sized, grass, adult pitch. Flags and nets are stored on site in a lock up. Dublin City Council makes the call as to whether the pitch is playable in winter or not.

  4. Parking – Parking is available outside the park along the side of the road. It is a residential area so consideration should be made when parking.
